History

Established in 1979, Syeda's caffee traces its roots to the vibrant streets of chennai. Founded by a trio of coffee enthusiasts—Sarah, Alex, and Chris—each cup poured at Syeda's caffee tells a story of passion and dedication to the craft. The idea sparked during a backpacking adventure through South America, where the founders were inspired by the diverse coffee cultures they encountered.

The journey from a small corner spot to its current location on Laenra road was marked by quirky interior transformations. The walls showcase a mural painted by a local artist, depicting the evolution of coffee from bean to cup. With a commitment to sustainability, the shop proudly sources its beans from a collective of small-scale farmers, ensuring a fair and direct trade partnership.

Over the years, Syed's Cafe has become more than a coffee destination; it's a community hub. Weekly open mic nights, book club meetings, and a cozy fireplace contribute to the shop's warm and inclusive atmosphere. With a mission to make every visitor feel like a part of the Syeda's caffee family, the journey continues to evolve, one cup at a time.
